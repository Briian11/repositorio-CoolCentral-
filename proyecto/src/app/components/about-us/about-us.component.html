Founders and History:

In the year 2018, in the picturesque neighborhood of Ruzafa in Valencia, four visionary friends decided to combine their passions for urban fashion to bring CoolCentral to life. Víctor Tardón, Brian Torre, Santiago Cerezo, and Iván Hernándiz shared a common dream: to create a space where fashion was more than just a trend, where it became a form of authentic and personal expression.

From its humble beginnings in a small local shop, CoolCentral quickly found a place in the hearts of Valencians. The store became a meeting point for the community, where creativity and individuality were celebrated and encouraged.

CoolCentral's success did not go unnoticed, and it soon expanded beyond the borders of Valencia. Today, our stores are not only found in the bustling capital of Turia but also in the vibrant cities of Seville, Madrid, Barcelona, and Bilbao. As we continue our journey, we remain true to our core values of innovation, quality, and authenticity, bringing the spirit of CoolCentral to every corner of Spain.
